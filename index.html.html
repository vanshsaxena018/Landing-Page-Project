Our Features

Quality Tables and Balls

A premium snooker table crafted from the finest materials, ensuring a smooth and precise playing surface. The table features professional-grade cushions for accurate rebound and durable pockets designed to last and Our snooker balls are made to the highest standards, offering perfect roundness and weight consistency.

Cheap Cafe

Chep Cafe, nestled within the vibrant atmosphere of the Snooker Club, offers a delightful dining experience for both snooker enthusiasts and casual visitors. The cafe provides a cozy and relaxed ambiance, making it an ideal spot to unwind after a game or simply enjoy a meal with friends.

Monthly Snooker Tournaments

We host monthly snooker tournaments designed to bring together enthusiasts and players of all skill levels for exciting and competitive events. Our goal is to create a vibrant community around the sport, fostering camaraderie, skill development, and a love for snooker.

About Us

Welcome to The Cue Club, the premier destination for snooker enthusiasts in Lucknow. Founded with a passion for the game and a commitment to fostering a vibrant community, our club offers an unparalleled experience for players of all levels. Whether you are a seasoned player or just starting out, you will find a welcoming environment, quality facilities, and ample opportunities to enjoy and excel at snooker. At The Cue Club, our mission is to provide a top-tier snooker experience that combines affordability, quality, and community. We strive to create a space where players can come together to enjoy the game, improve their skills, and build lasting friendships. The Cue Club is more than just a place to play snooker; it's a community. We believe in fostering connections among our members, and we regularly organize social events and gatherings to help players network, share tips, and build friendships. Our club is a place where you can feel at home, whether you're here to play, learn, or simply enjoy the company of fellow snooker enthusiasts.
